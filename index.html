I can create meaningful designs for you.

I am a travel agent turned Medical biller turned designer! Talk about iterations, and my life is one.

After several years in Medical billing I found myself needing a change and a desire to find a more creative outlet for myself.

UX design appealed to me because it embodies many elements of the medical field such as empathy, user centric approach and understanding the needs and motivations of others.

I continue to have a passionate interest in healthcare and welcome the opportunity to engage in healthcare related projects.

I like helping people. Making a product usable and fun to use is my way of helping others meet their needs in a meaningful and efficient way.

I feel any UX project is incomplete without research. All my projects begin with thorough user research. I like to gather data through survey, interviews and graphs.

Qualitative Interview

Good interview questions are very important for any user interview. I have learned to ask a lot of why’s. It’s a great way to empathize with your user.

Empathy

I truly believe showing empathy towards a user helps me understand what they really want. It helps me connect with my users. User journey, Storyboards, user personas are my strengths to show Empathy.
